Why the push up is the best exercise?

The push up is an ancient exercise practiced all over the world, from Greece to India; the push up has been used to both conditioning and strenght training, from military training to sports training, push ups give work to the vast mayority to muscles in our body emphasizing the chest, shoulder and triceps, some back and the core muscles for stabilization, is an exercise that can be done anywhere, anytime doesn't require equipment and is one of the most basic exercise we can do to get in shape, and it's part of the three basics:

  • The pull up
  • The push ups
  • The squat

      • With just these three exercises we can get in tremendous shape, this already has been demostrated by calisthenics athletes like Hannibal for king with his famous routine "around the world" which consist in doing the three basics in a row with the addition of "dips"
